Serving the Sea's Finest Since 2008

Océanique was founded by Chef Priya Nair — a Mangalore-born culinary artist trained in Marseille and Tokyo. Inspired by the vibrant fish markets of India's Konkan coast, she brought the world's finest seafood traditions to Hyderabad.

Every morning, our procurement team speaks directly with fishermen at the Visakhapatnam and Goa harbours. Only the freshest catch — verified for sustainability certifications — makes it to our kitchen by noon.

We believe coastal food is an act of storytelling — the tide, the fisherman, the fire, and you.
